## Quick Reference

**Best rounded + sweet spot:**
- **#1 Santa Teresa 1796** - vanilla, honey, chocolate
- **#2 El Dorado 12** - tropical, toffee, molasses
- **#3 Brugal XV** - butterscotch, tropical

**Naturally rounded (0-6 g/L):**
- Havana Club SdM - caramel, cocoa, spice
- Doorly's XO - banana, raisins, sherry

**Avoid:**
- A.H. Riise 1888 (85-96 g/L)
- Don Papa Masskara (additives)
