## Detailed Flavor Profiles

### Santa Teresa 1796 (Your #1)
**Nose:** Coffee, dark chocolate, caramel, vanilla, plums, honey
**Palate:** Milk chocolate, vanilla, honey, toffee, cashews, butterscotch
**Finish:** Medium, warm, peppery
**Character:** "Smooth, round, silky texture"

### El Dorado 12
**Nose:** Tropical fruit, spice, honey, dark sugar
**Palate:** Dark chocolate, tobacco, caramel, dates, raisins
**Finish:** Elegant, dry, hint of oak
**Character:** Rich Demerara, full-bodied

### Brugal XV
**Nose:** Butterscotch, tropical fruit, vanilla
**Palate:** Light, smooth, tropical notes, oak
**Finish:** Clean, light sweetness
**Character:** Light, approachable, PX sherry influence

### Havana Club Selección de Maestros
**Nose:** Caramel, brown sugar, apricot, peach, nutmeg
**Palate:** Cocoa, coffee, tobacco, brown spices, cherry
**Finish:** Long, spiced, smoky
**Character:** Naturally rounded, complex Cuban

### Doorly's XO
**Nose:** Caramel, banana, raisins, apricot
**Palate:** Toffee, walnut, citrus, nutmeg, pepper
**Finish:** Medium-long, dried fruit, pepper
**Character:** Balanced Barbadian, subtle sherry
